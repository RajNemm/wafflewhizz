Our Story

Born from a Passion for the Perfect Waffle

WaffleWhizz started in 2024 in the heart of Visakhapatnam with one simple obsession — making the crispiest, fluffiest, most indulgent waffles in the region. What began as a single outlet has now blossomed into a beloved brand with 10+ locations across Andhra Pradesh & Telangana.

Every waffle we make is a promise — golden on the outside, impossibly soft on the inside, loaded with the finest toppings. We believe great food brings people together, one perfect square at a time.
